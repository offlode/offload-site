Built for the way businesses actually use laundry

How a business account works

  1. Tell us about your operation — locations, volume, pickup frequency, special handling. Takes 5 minutes.
  2. We build your route plan — pickup windows, dropoff windows, your dedicated laundromat partner(s).
  3. Sign the master service agreement — volume rate locked in, monthly invoicing on Net-15 terms.
  4. Pickups start within 5 business days — first pickup is on us. Your account manager monitors weeks 1-4 closely.
  5. You get a monthly invoice — itemized by location, by day, by service type. Pay by ACH, wire, or card.
