Laundry Pickup & Delivery Pricing Guide

Transparent, flat-rate pricing. Know exactly what you'll pay before you order. No surprises, no per-pound guessing games.

Bag Pricing — Simple and Transparent

Offload uses flat-rate bag pricing, not per-pound pricing. You choose a bag size, and you know exactly what you'll pay. If your laundry goes slightly over the weight limit, it's just $2.50 per additional pound — and you'll be notified of the final weight after processing.

Bag Size Weight Limit Flat Price Best For
Small Bag Up to 10 lbs $24.99 Singles, 1 week of basics
Medium Bag ⭐ Most Popular Up to 20 lbs $44.99 Couples, small families
Large Bag Up to 30 lbs $59.99 Families, big loads
XL Bag Up to 50 lbs $89.99 Bulk, commercial, Airbnb

Overage rate: $2.50 per additional pound beyond your bag's weight limit. You'll always see the final weight and total before your card is charged.
