How It Works in NYC

  1. Enter your address on our website or app — you'll see if you're in our service area instantly.
  2. Choose your bag size and delivery speed. We show you the exact price before you commit.
  3. Schedule a pickup — same-day available, or choose a time that works for you.
  4. A vetted driver picks up your laundry from your door. Track their location in real time on a live map.
  5. We wash, dry, and fold at a certified laundromat partner near you.
  6. Fresh laundry delivered back — folded and packaged. Contactless delivery available.

The entire process takes as little as 12 hours with Same Day delivery, or 48 hours with free Standard delivery.
