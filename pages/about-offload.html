About Offload

We're a technology company solving laundry. Our mission is to give everyone access to clean clothes without the time, effort, or hassle of doing it themselves.

Our Mission

Laundry is the chore nobody likes but everyone has to do. It eats 5-7 hours per week for the average person — sorting, hauling, waiting, folding. In a city like New York, where space is tight, laundromats are crowded, and time is everything, we saw an opportunity to do something better.

Offload exists to eliminate laundry as a chore. We connect you with certified local laundromat partners and vetted drivers who pick up, clean, and deliver your laundry — on your schedule, at a price you know upfront.

Technology-First Approach

Unlike most laundry services that are essentially phone-call-based or simple marketplaces, Offload is built on a modern technology stack designed for reliability and transparency:

Smart Vendor Matching

When you place an order, our system considers multiple factors — laundromat capacity, specialization, geographic proximity, and current load — to find the best match.

Automated Scheduling

We track order patterns by neighborhood and day of week to coordinate drivers and prepare laundromat partners for incoming volume.

Quality Monitoring

Every laundromat partner has a quality score based on on-time rates, customer ratings, dispute frequency, and quality check results.

16-State Order Tracking

From order placement to delivery, every step is tracked with timestamps and photos. You always know exactly where your laundry stands.

Our Values

  • Transparency — Know your price before you order. Get photo proof at every handoff. Status updates at each step.
  • Trust — Background-checked drivers, certified laundromats, industry-leading garment protection ($250/item).
  • Technology — Automated scheduling, smart vendor matching, quality monitoring, and a website-based chat assistant.
  • Fairness — Fair pay for drivers ($8.50+/trip + tips). Fair revenue share for laundromat partners (65%). Fair pricing for customers.
