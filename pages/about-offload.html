About Offload

We're a technology company solving laundry. Our mission is to give everyone access to clean clothes without the time, effort, or hassle of doing it themselves.

Our Mission

Laundry is the chore nobody likes but everyone has to do. It eats 5-7 hours per week for the average person — sorting, hauling, waiting, folding. In a city like New York, where space is tight, laundromats are crowded, and time is everything, we saw an opportunity to do something better.

Offload exists to eliminate laundry as a chore. We connect you with certified local laundromat partners and vetted drivers who pick up, clean, and deliver your laundry — on your schedule, at a price you know upfront.

Technology-First Approach

Unlike most laundry services that are essentially phone-call-based or simple marketplaces, Offload is built on a modern technology stack with AI deeply integrated into every aspect of the operation:

AI Vendor Matching

When you place an order, our algorithm considers 12+ factors — laundromat capacity, specialization, quality score, geographic proximity, current load — to find the best match.

Predictive Demand Forecasting

We model order patterns by neighborhood, day of week, and weather to pre-position drivers and prepare laundromat partners for incoming volume.

Real-Time Quality Scoring

Every laundromat partner has an AI-generated health score based on on-time rates, customer ratings, dispute frequency, and quality check results.

16-State Order Tracking

From order placement to delivery, every step is tracked with GPS, timestamps, and photos. You always know exactly where your laundry is.

Our Values

  • Transparency — Know your price before you order. See your driver on a map. Get photo proof at every handoff.
  • Trust — Background-checked drivers, certified laundromats, industry-leading garment protection ($250/item).
  • Technology — AI-powered everything: chatbot support, voice ordering, smart routing, quality prediction.
  • Fairness — Fair pay for drivers ($8.50+/trip + tips). Fair revenue share for laundromat partners (65%). Fair pricing for customers.
