Laundry Service for Busy Professionals

You didn't build your career to spend Saturday at the laundromat. Offload handles your laundry so you can focus on what matters.

The Time Math

The average New Yorker spends 5-7 hours per week on laundry — sorting, carrying, washing, drying, folding, putting away. Over a year, that's 300+ hours. That's almost two full work weeks spent on laundry.

With Offload, your time investment is:

  • 30 seconds to place an order (no account needed)
  • 0 seconds carrying, washing, or waiting
  • 10 seconds to grab your delivery from the door

That's 5+ hours of your life back, every single week.

Built for the Way You Work

Order from Anywhere

Place an order from the subway, from a meeting, from bed at midnight. Our website and app work everywhere, and the virtual assistant helps if you're in a rush.

Contactless Everything

Leave your bag outside the door. We pick it up, clean it, and deliver it back — you don't even need to be home. Perfect for long office days.

One-Tap Reorder

Same order as last time? One tap. Your preferences, bag size, and delivery speed are saved. Reordering takes literally one second.

Subscription Auto-Pilot

Set up a weekly subscription and never think about laundry again. We'll pick up on your schedule, every week, automatically.

Perfect for These Professionals

  • Tech workers pulling long hours — clean clothes delivered before you get home
  • Healthcare workers on rotating shifts — schedule pickups around your schedule, not the other way around
  • Finance professionals who need crisp shirts daily — dry cleaning and wash & fold in one order
  • Freelancers and remote workers who don't want to interrupt their flow to do laundry
  • Parents juggling work and family — let us handle the laundry mountain so you can be present
  • Airbnb hosts who need fast turnaround between guests — same-day delivery available

What It Costs

Less than you think. A Medium Bag (up to 20 lbs) is $44.99 with free standard delivery. That covers a full week's laundry for most people. If you subscribe, it drops to $40.49 with the Basic plan or $38.24 with Plus.

Compare that to your hourly rate and the decision makes itself.
