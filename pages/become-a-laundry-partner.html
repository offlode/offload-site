Become a Laundry Partner

Get a steady stream of orders without the marketing headache. Our platform handles scheduling, payments, and customer communication — you focus on quality.

How It Works for Partners

  1. Apply online — tell us about your laundromat, equipment, capacity, and services.
  2. Get verified — we visit your location, verify quality standards, and onboard you.
  3. Start receiving orders — our system matches nearby customer orders to your shop based on capacity and specialization.
  4. Process and earn — wash, dry, fold. Get paid weekly via Stripe direct deposit.

What You Get

Steady Order Flow

No more slow days. Our system distributes orders based on your capacity so you always have work, never overwhelmed.

Smart Queue Management

Smart order prioritization based on SLA deadlines, load type, and your equipment. Maximize throughput without the headache.

Bluetooth Scale Integration

Connect your scale and weights are recorded automatically — no manual entry, no disputes about weight.

Automated Payouts

Weekly direct deposits via Stripe Connect. See your earnings in real time on the partner dashboard.

Performance Dashboard

Track your quality score, on-time rate, customer ratings, and earnings. See exactly how you're doing.

Zero Marketing Spend

We bring the customers. You focus on what you do best — cleaning clothes.
